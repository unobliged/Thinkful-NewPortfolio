About Me

Although I did not pursue programming as a career earlier in life, I had always had an interest. I learned some compsci in high school, which really came in handy after I became an analyst (VB/VBA sadly). Before going to MIT for my MBA, I had started learning ruby to pursue my side project of making language learning software for Japanese and Chinese, which later became the focus of my Entrepreneurship Track studies and 100K entries. I am now focused on becoming the best developer I can and learning how developers work together as a team to take a product from idea to market.
